Home made Chicken Pizza

Post by Confidence Ogbonna

Nelson Foods is a local, vertically-integrated, family-owned and operated chicken meat company producing and selling organic chicken meat products. We focus our sales and marketing emphasis on Chicken, Turkey and eggs to local suppliers and local customers, all in an effort to help feed the nation. Only Family raised and source-verified poultry produce are selected for our marketing program, and are then fed a high-quality, 100% organic diet consisting of thyme and oregano . Our poultry are carefully processed at an inspected facility near Etche, Rivers.
